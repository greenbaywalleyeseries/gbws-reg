Tournament Format

If possible, qualifying tournaments will be held on Sundays. The championship is a two day (Saturday & Sunday) event. Tournament hours will run from 7 AM to 3:15 PM (2 flights). Each tournament awards 500 points to the top team in a Catch-Record-Release format. Each place after 1st decreases by 2 points for all teams that register fish. 100 points are awarded to teams fishing the tournament that do not register any fish.

Option Pot

Teams may enter an option pot for each tournament. The cost is $200 per team with 100% payback. The option pot pays 1 in 4 to teams participating in the option pot.


Bass Pro/Cabela's Big Fish

Teams may enter an option pot for each tournament. The cost is $200 per team with 100% payback. The option pot pays 1 in 4 to teams participating in the option pot.

NTC Qualifier

Green Bay Walleye Series tournaments are qualifying events for the Cabela's NTC. To be eligible for the Cabela's NTC bid, both team members must be registered with The Walleye Federation (TWF).

To register as members of the TWF, contact the Walleye Federation at (580) 765-9031 and inform them you would like to become a TWF member through the Green Bay Walleye Series. If you are a current member of the TWF through another club, you do not need to register with the TWF through the GBWS.

Registration Information

Registration cost is $400 per team for regular season qualifiers and $500 for the Championship tournament. There is also an option pot ($200 entry fee) as well as Bass Pro/Cabela's Big Fish Pot ($20 entry fee) for each tournament. Teams must be members of the Green Bay Walleye Series.

Championship Qualification

To qualify for the GBWS championship, teams must earn 800 points or fish all three qualifying tournaments.
